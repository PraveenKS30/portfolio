About Me

Engineering Leader. AI Enthusiast. Builder.

I'm a Senior Engineering Manager at Optum (UnitedHealth Group), where I lead 30+ onshore and offshore engineers building healthcare technology that impacts millions of patients across the U.S. clinical domain. Over 11+ years at Optum — growing from Senior Software Engineer to Engineering Manager to my current role — I've developed deep expertise in architecting scalable systems and driving AI-powered transformation in healthcare.

My passion lies at the intersection of AI and healthcare: from leading Generative AI initiatives using LlamaIndex, OpenAI Agents SDK, and Google ADK, to mentoring teams to adopt modern engineering practices. I believe the best code is code that helps someone get healthier, faster.

Outside of Optum, I run TechyTacos on YouTube (8K+ subscribers), sharing tutorials on AI/ML, cloud, and backend engineering. I also write on Medium and contribute to open-source projects focused on Generative AI and cloud platforms.
